Hussaini Mustapha Abdulkadir (MBDFM)

Chief Executive Officer

Hussaini Mustapha Abdulkadir is an agri-business professional, data-driven field operator, and sustainability advocate with a strong foundation in microbiology and advanced training in management, public health, and finance. He blends scientific insight with business strategy to design, implement, and scale sustainable agricultural systems that drive productivity, strengthen rural economies, and support national food security.

As the Chief Executive Officer of AgroGreenEconomy Ltd, Hussaini leads a fully integrated agricultural company focused on crop production, agro-manufacturing, livestock systems, and fertilizer innovation. Under his leadership, the organization continues to promote climate-smart agriculture, empower farming communities, and advance eco-friendly solutions—supported by his personal commitment to environmental restoration, including over 200 trees planted across Nigeria. Hussaini also serves as a Marketing Manager at RUSBI Group of Companies, where he oversees market research, campaign execution, stakeholder engagement, and data-backed decision-making. His work strengthens brand visibility, improves operational efficiency, and ensures measurable impact in both digital and physical markets. His earlier experience includes roles with the Federal Airports Authority of Nigeria (FAAN) and the International Institute for Tropical Agriculture (IITA), where he developed strong competence in field data collection, monitoring & evaluation, reporting, and operational compliance. These roles refined his expertise in data integrity, project documentation, quality assurance, and field supervision within development-driven environments.

Academically, Hussaini holds a B.Sc. in Microbiology, a Postgraduate Diploma in Management, and MBA in Finance & Investment at Bayero University’s Dangote Business School, with an additional ongoing masters in Public Health. Meanwhile his also a full membership of the institution of bussiness diplomacy and finance development ,His multidisciplinary training positions him at the intersection of agriculture, public health, sustainability, and financial strategy. A proactive leader and communicator, Hussaini is passionate about climate responsibility, entrepreneurial development, innovation in food systems, and the use of data for informed agricultural transformation. He is multilingual—fluent in English, Hausa, and Arabic—and continues to support youth empowerment and community-driven sustainability initiatives.
